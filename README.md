# 👥 HR Analytics Dashboard | Power BI

## 📌 Project Overview

The **HR Analytics Dashboard** is an interactive Business Intelligence solution developed using **Microsoft Power BI** to analyze workforce performance, employee attrition, and key Human Resource metrics. The dashboard enables HR professionals and business leaders to monitor workforce trends, identify the primary drivers of employee turnover, and support strategic decision-making through interactive visualizations and data-driven insights.

The project follows a structured analytical approach consisting of three dashboard pages that answer the key business questions: **What is happening? Why is it happening? What actions should be taken?**

---

## 🎯 Business Objectives

* Monitor workforce composition and employee distribution.
* Measure and analyze employee attrition across departments and job roles.
* Identify key factors influencing employee turnover.
* Evaluate workforce performance and employee satisfaction.
* Support HR decision-making using interactive analytics.
* Improve employee retention through data-driven insights.

---

# 📊 Dashboard Pages

## 📄 Page 1 – Workforce & Attrition Overview

### Business Question

**What is the current workforce status, and where is employee attrition occurring?**

### Analysis Includes

* Total Employees
* Active Employees
* Attrition Count
* Attrition Rate (%)
* Average Tenure
* Average Monthly Income
* Attrition by Department
* Attrition by Job Role
* Workforce Age Distribution
* Attrition by Gender

---

## 📄 Page 2 – Employee Attrition Drivers

### Business Question

**What factors contribute to employee attrition?**

### Analysis Includes

* Overtime vs Attrition
* Job Satisfaction vs Attrition
* Monthly Income vs Attrition
* Attrition by Years at Company
* Employee Risk Analysis
* Workforce Behavior Analysis

---

## 📄 Page 3 – Retention & Workforce Performance

### Business Question

**How can employee retention and workforce performance be improved?**

### Analysis Includes

* Salary Hike by Department
* Average Monthly Income by Education Field
* Performance Rating Distribution
* Years Since Last Promotion vs Attrition
* Workforce Development Analysis
* Employee Retention Insights
